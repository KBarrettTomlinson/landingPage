Murmuration


The Table Topics Practice App is a full-stack web application which facilitates groups in practicing off the cuff responses to themed questions in a low stakes and supportive environment.

Easy to establish table topics style sessions with a database of themes and questions, participants can easily join via their phone while the app chooses speakers and questions at random. The app provides visual feedback indicating the length of time each participant has spoken, as well as the overall success of the group.

Murmuration: A Table Topics Practice Group
